## How to Use

1. Open the Workshop in the Station
2. Navigate to the **"Agents Individual Items"** tab
3. Research the specific item kit you need (10 credits)
4. Purchase kits (5 credits each)
5. Add kits to your dropship loadout
6. Land on the planet - **kits survive!**
7. Consume the kit from your inventory
8. The item appears in your inventory!
